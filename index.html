About our Teachers

Elizabeth Haslet

Founder - Velveteen Hippo Project

Elizabeth Haslet a homeschool mom of four who sells art under The Velveteen Hippo Project. Her special love is watching kids light up with a fire to make their own things. She believes everyone is creative in their own way and that practice improves everything.

Timothy Haslet

Professional Artist

Timothy Haslet is a professional local artist, father of four, with a B.A. in fine arts from Whitworth. He is part of Coupeville's Penn Cove Gallery. He specializes in oil painting but loves working with kids to find their own gifts. Teaching runs in his family, and he loves to share what he knows with others. He is the founder of Open Studio, a group that invites artists to get together, make art and encourage one another in their art journey.

Alicia McGaha

Art Teacher

Alicia McGaha has taught many art classes to children over the years. She is a truly gifted oil painter and mom of three with a passion to make art accessible to all ages. She also uses art to raise awareness for special needs issues and to help kids make art on a budget. It doesn't have to be expensive to be amazing.
